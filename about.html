About UniqueStore

UniqueStore is Nepal's premier online shopping destination, dedicated to bringing you the best products at competitive prices with exceptional customer service. Founded in 2023, we have grown to become one of the most trusted e-commerce platforms in Nepal, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the country.

Our mission is to make online shopping accessible, convenient, and enjoyable for everyone in Nepal. We believe that quality products should be available to all, regardless of location, and we work tirelessly to ensure fast and reliable delivery to every corner of our beautiful country.

Our Values

  • Customer satisfaction is our top priority
  • Quality products from trusted brands and suppliers
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden costs
  • Fast and reliable delivery nationwide
  • Secure payment options for peace of mind
  • Supporting local businesses and entrepreneurs
  • Environmental responsibility in packaging and operations

Our Story

UniqueStore began as a small dream in 2023 when our founders recognized the need for a reliable, customer-focused e-commerce platform in Nepal. Starting from a small office in Kathmandu with just five team members, we were determined to bridge the gap between quality products and Nepali consumers.

Through dedication, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction, we have grown into Nepal's most trusted online marketplace. Today, we serve customers across all 77 districts, partnering with local and international brands to bring you the best products at competitive prices.

Our journey continues as we expand our services, improve our technology, and strengthen our commitment to making online shopping accessible and enjoyable for every Nepali family.
